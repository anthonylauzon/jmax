Beta-Testing

After being tested at the production at IRCAM jMax will get very soon in a second beta-test phase, which will be open to everybody. This will happen first for the SGI version and a little later for other platforms.

If you are interested in getting a beta-test version of jMax, please let us know by sending us an email.

Please tell us something about who you are and . . .

  • What you are doing?
  • What's the framework?
  • What machines you are using?

The beta-test version (of course) will be free, just like the following distributions of jMax.
