Hello,

You can download the first beta release (2.3.15) of jMax for Linux
(Intel) platforms. Please visit jMax home page: http://www.ircam.fr/jmax
and follow instructions for download.

This release has been compiled for Pentium-Pro and Pentium-II processors
and uses glibc. It has been tested on Redhat 5.1 and 5.2.

Please notes that the restriction in the license forbidding the
redistribution of the binaries is relative only to the beta status,
and will be removed in the non-beta release.

If you download and test jMax on Linux, we will be happy to hear from
you; please, subscribe to the jMax mailing list (instructions in 
http://www.ircam.fr/jmax).

Installation instructions are included in the package.
