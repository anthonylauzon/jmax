General questions about jMax

What does the j in jMax stand for?
jMax is short for the complex term Time + j Max.
The imaginary part determined the name, while the real part defines the constrains...
(One could also think that it's because the user interface of jMax is entirely written in Java.)

What is FTS?
FTS was always the name of the server of the real-time project at IRCAM and the former application developped here was refered to as Max/FTS. Since the server part of jMax is still FTS, and FTS is shorter to spell than "the jMax server" we keep on saying FTS meaning the fast, real-time, number crunshing, C written half of jMax.
