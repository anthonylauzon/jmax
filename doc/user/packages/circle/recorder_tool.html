Recorder Tool

With this tool you can record and playback a trajectory of a source (or more sources) in the time, i.e. their changes in position , aperture and orientation. The recording is done at a "sampling rate" that you can change via the "Recorder Sampling Rate" menu (submenu of the menu "Options"). The same for the playing.

When you mount this tool a set of buttons is shown at the top right of the window. If you click on the "rec" button you can start a recording session. When clicking on a source, the movements and changes in aperture/orientation are recorded, and a trajectory is drawn in the background with a node for every sample. The recording stops at mouseUp. Now you can:

  • continue the recording with another (or the same) source by clicking and moving
  • click on the "pause" button , then move a source without recording the changes
  • modify the trajectory by selecting/removing/adding/modifying the nodes of the trajectory
  • click on the "reset" button to delete the trajectory
  • click on the "ok" button to validate this trajectory , exit from the record mode and activate the "play" button

When you click on the "play" button you start a thread that play the trajectory with the current sampling rate and sends out the corresponding messages.

At this point, click on the "rec" button to modify the existing trajectory. Recording and playing are performed by different threads, so you can play and record two different trajectory at the same time.
