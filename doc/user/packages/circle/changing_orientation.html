Changing Source Orientation

Orientation changing can be done in two ways:

  • by clicking on the source and then dragging with ALT_KEY pressed (the source will be oriented from the source-center to the mouse location)
  • by ALT-LEFT/ ALT-RIGHT Keys (increment/decrement of one deg) , or by ALT-SHIFT-LEFT/ ALT-SHIFT-RIGHT (increment/decrement faster)
