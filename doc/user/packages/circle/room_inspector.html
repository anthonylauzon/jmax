Room Inspector Dialog

Double clicking on a room open a dialog showing all the room property values. You can change this values and , clicking on the ok-button, you can assign them to the room.

You can change:

  • the room name, by writing them in the "room name" EditField
  • the room color. Clicking on the "colors" button open a ColorChooser dialog
  • the template associated to the room. Clicking on the "template choser" button open a FileChooser
  • the radius by dragging on the corresponding slider
  • the drop mode.Clicking on the "drop mode" comboBox you can choose beetwen "logarithmic" and " linear" mode , and dragging on the slider you can change the parameter value
  • the buffer zone presence attenuation parameter by dragging on the corresponding slider. You can also activate/desactivate the buffer zone clicking on the corresponding radioButton
  • the room presence , running reverberance , late reverberance , heaviness and liveness psychoacoustic parameters, by dragging on the corresponding sliders

You can also activate/desactivate the room by clicking on the "activated" radioButton at the bottom of the dialog.
