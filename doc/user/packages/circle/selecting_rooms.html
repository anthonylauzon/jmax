Selecting a Room

Area selection is obtained by mouse drag outside objects.

A room can be selected either by area selection or by clicking in it.

Selection can be extended or reduced by SHIFT modifier: SHIFT-selecting (either by click or area select) an already selected source deselects it, SHIFT-selecting a not selected source selects it.

Also is possible to select a room shape node , by clicking on it. The use of the SHIFT modifier is the same than in the room selection.
