Changing Source Aperture

Aperture changing can be done in two ways:

  • by clicking on the source and then dragging with CTRL-ALT Keys pressed (dragging upper will increment and dragging lower will decrement aperture)
  • by CTRL-ALT-UP/ CTRL-ALT-DOWN Keys (increment/decrement of one deg) , or by CTRL-ALT-SHIFT-UP/ CTRL-ALT-SHIFT-DOWN (increment/decrement faster)
