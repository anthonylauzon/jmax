Changing Source Type

You can toggle a source from Local Type to Receiver Type and viceversa by CTRL-CLICK on it. When a source become a receiver, her graphic representation changes (green with a white cross inside). When you define a new receiver the old receiver become a local source. If you are in "relative to receiver representation mode" the representation changes relatively to new receiver. If there isn't a receiver source, we are in absolute mode and the center of the sound scene is the center of the background green cross.
