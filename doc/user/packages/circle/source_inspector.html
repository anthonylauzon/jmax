Source Inspector Dialog

Double clicking on a source opens a dialog showing all the source property values (only with ArrowTool and RecorderTool). You can change these values and, by clicking on the ok-button, you can assign them to the source.

You can change:

  • the source name, by writing them in the "source name" EditField (the source will show the first character of the name)
  • the source color. Clicking on the "colors" button open a ColorChooser dialog
  • the source type. Clicking on the "source type" comboBox you can choose beetwen "local" and "receiver" type
  • the moving mode.Clicking on the "moving mode" comboBox you can choose beetwen "static" , " dynamic" and "centered" mode
  • the presence , warmth , brillance and envelopment psychoacoustic parameters, by dragging on the corresponding sliders
  • the cartesian and polar coordinates (in absolute mode and in relative to the receiver mode) , the orientation and the aperture , by entering them in the relative editFields
  • the template associated to the source. Clicking on the "template chooser" button open a FileChooser

You can also activate/desactivate the source by clicking on the "activated" radioButton at the bottom of the dialog.
